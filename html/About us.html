About us:



Pakistan Telecommunication Company Limited (PTCL) is the largest telecommunication company in Pakistan. The Government of Pakistan sold 26% shares and control of the company to Etisalat in 2006. PTCL looked like an ideal candidate to feed investors’ seemingly insatiable appetite for telecom shares. “I imagined it would take about 15 months to put away — nine months for the preparation phase and six months to execute,” recalls Charles Manby, the London-based head of Goldman’s global telecom group.
